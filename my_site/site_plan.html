Overview

Purpose

Have you recognized that Idaho often has wonderful, and even colorful sunsets and sunrises? Or perhaps just a breath taking view in nature while on a hiking trip? Pictures of these stunning sights can bring peace, positivity, and awe when gazing at them, which I myself watch out for often. Whenever I recognize a sight that can bring any of these relative feelings, I whip out my camera and take a photo to the best of my ability. With a photography website I can not only share the things and views I see around me but also could have the opportunity to sell my art in printed forms such as canvas or high quality image printing according to the customers desire.

Who knows, maybe at one point I can let it be a more versatile website and other photographers can share their shots and works of art!

Audience

For those that love to see photos of color, beauty, awe, wonder, thought, and more can explore this site to find photos they can admire. With permission and/or credit given, customers can use some of the photos as screensavers/device backgrounds or other similar features for continual inspiration. Other photographers may submit their photos and they may be posted with their credentials shown.
