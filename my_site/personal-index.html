Hi there! My name is Dillan Rawlings (hence the DR in my logo). I have made this website for all and anyone to see my photos I have taken over the years. Nature has almost always been something fascinating to my eyes and conscience. If and when I have the ability and oppurtunities to, I take out a camera (any camera-like an Smartphone) and do my best to snap the beauty I see and recognize. Sunsets in particular have always fascintated me with their glory. They are practicaly never the same every single day. I will admit, haha, at times they can be that simple "boring" yellow, but at least it is a sign over another new day to come. And when it comes to all else other than sunsets, I do my best to keep an eye out for simple and elegant, or extrordinary, or just plain fascinating and worthy to take a snap of. Although some photographs may not be the best, I did what I could with what I had/have.
